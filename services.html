Wild Roots Horsemanship

Services Offered and Pricing

  • Colt Starting

    • For 2 year olds and older who have had zero consistent training thus far especially under saddle. Colt starting projects are required to stay for at least 60 days (45 sessions) but are invited to stay up to 90 days (66 sessions)
    45 sessions - $1,845
    66 sessions - $2,769

  • Basic Training & Feral Projects

    • 45 day stay with 30 sessions of training. Any age, any discipline that needs some holes in their foundational skill set filled. This includes basics like standing tied, being responsive instead of reactive, standing for mounting/dismounting, having feet handled, carrying their rider in a balanced fashion, building confidence, etc.
    30 sessions - $1,385

  • Trail Training

    • 45 days of stay with 30 sessions of being out on the trail. Any age that would like to learn to be confident and content on the trail. Horses under 2 years will ONLY be hand-walked or ponied. This includes some obstacle training as well. Can include offsite rides (hauling out) for an additional fee.
    30 sessions - $1,385

  • Target Training

    • 30 days of stay with 22 sessions of training. Any age, any discipline that is experiencing a specific behavioral or training problem(s) that needs to be addressed.
    22 sessions - $1,025

  • Conditioning Program

    • 30 days of stay with 26 sessions of conditioning. For 3+ year olds that are broke to ride and need an exercise program to get into performance shape. This includes the three main areas of performance - flexibility, strength, and endurance.
    26 sessions - $1,025
